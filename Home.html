About Hootlums

Hootlums are a collection of 10,000 crypto-owl NFTs living on the Polygon Blockchain. They are all unique in their own way, but some are more rare than others. They wear T-shirts with prints that look familiar, but are just a little bit different. Some drink a beer, some are taking a selfie, some are playing the guitar or holding another object. The rarest of them all are the G20 country hoots, the golden hoots and the 5 special hoots. If you own a Hoot, you have a chance to win prizes in the periodic raffles.

The Raffle

After every 1,000 minted H​ootlums a raffle will take place. Your Hootlum will be your participation "ticket". The kind of Hootlum you own determines your winning chances.

The first raffles, after every 1,000 minted Hootlums, are held according to below weights. When all 10,000 Hootlums have been minted, raffles can be based on different properties (e.g. headwear, shirt print, object, etc.)

The minting price per Hootlum will increase after every 1,000 minted Hootlums. Along with that the raffle prize pot will increase too. 

Prizes of first raffle:
1st prize - 1 ETH
2nd prize - 0.2 ETH
3rd prize - 0.05 ETH

Prize pots are to increase with 0.5 ETH every raffle. The raffle setup can be changed according to input from the Hootlum community.

After all 10,000 Hootlums have been minted the prize pot of the raffles will depend on traded volume.

The setup of the raffle will be completely transparent and will be shared here along with the results.
